Terms of Service ✦

Soda_Pawp's TOS 2026 — please read it all, then confirm with me that you've read & agree! <3

Payment

  • Payment may be upfront or after the sketch — you choose!
  • This mainly applies to PayPal (they take service fees). If fees apply to your payment method, please make sure I still receive the proper amount after fees. (Ex: if a comm costs $15, I should still receive $15 after fees.)
  • This is why Zelle & Venmo are preferred!

Process

Note: Please don't provide AI-generated reference images. I'm happy to work with photos, descriptions, moodboards, etc. if you don't have a reference for your character!

  1. Tell me the size (ex. halfbody) and provide references for the character(s) you'd like drawn. Note if you'd also like add-ons (background, props).
  2. I'll confirm the commission and price.
  3. Tell me any additional info: desired expression, pose, etc.
  4. Pay before or after the sketch is completed — your choice.
  5. By default I send updates after each checkpoint (sketch, lineart, coloring, shading…) to confirm and ask if you'd like edits!
    • If you don't want updates after each stage, let me know! This may speed things up since I can keep going without waiting for a reply.
  6. Commission is done! Sent through email, Discord, and/or Instagram.
  • After each update I'm happy to make edits within reason! (Please don't ask for ~5 edits after I've moved on to the next stage…)
  • Completion times may vary based on my schedule — sometimes same-day, sometimes a few days to start. I'll also keep my status on my Trello (ex. "working on commissions!" or "finals week, comms on hold!"), so feel free to check there.

Cancellation

  • Before your commission is started: cancel any time! I get that things come up. (If this happens more than once, I may be unlikely to accept a future request.)
  • After the sketch is complete: I always confirm the commission before I start the sketch — so if you confirm, then ask to cancel right after I finish it, I may blacklist you. If you have a genuinely valid reason, please let me know!
  • Likewise, I reserve the right to cancel by fully refunding you at any time — this would only ever be due to negative personal circumstances, never a trivial reason. As compensation you'll get two extra art-raffle tickets and a spot near the front of the queue when commissions reopen.
  • If you've read this far, include the burger emoji in your confirmation message.

Usage

  • Totally happy if you use the piece on your social media — just please don't claim it as your own! (Tagging or crediting me is awesome but not required.)
  • Commissions are for personal use. Commercial use may be discussed for purchase.
  • Please let me know if you'd not like the finished commission posted on my socials! Otherwise I may use it as an example for commission posts or to display the art. :)
Thank you! 🍔
